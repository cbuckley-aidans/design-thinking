DESIGN PROCESS

Empathise

Understanding the needs and challenges of your users through research and observation.

Define

Clearly articulating the problem based on user needs and insights.

Ideate

Generating a wide range of creative solutions to address the defined problem.

Prototype

Building representations of potential solutions to test and refine.

TinkerCad Prototyping

Creating 3D models and prototypes using the TinkerCad platform.

3D Slicing

Preparing 3D models for printing by converting them into printable layers.
