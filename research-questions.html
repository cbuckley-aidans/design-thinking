Example Response

Sample response for "Newton House":

My House is named after Sir Isaac Newton, who was an English mathematician, physicist, and astronomer who lived from 1643 to 1727. He is known for developing the laws of motion and universal gravitation, which remained the foundation of physics for centuries. One of his most important contributions was his work in optics, where he discovered that white light is made up of all the colors of the rainbow. People remember him because his scientific discoveries revolutionized our understanding of the physical world and laid the groundwork for modern physics and mathematics.
